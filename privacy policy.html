Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11 2025

At Tasty Trills, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect from users, how we use it, who we may share it with, and the steps we take to protect your data. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide a smooth, personalized, and secure user experience.

a) Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery or reservation address
  • Special instructions or preferences

b) Payment Information

We do not store your full payment details. Payment transactions are processed securely via third-party gateways (e.g., Stripe, Razorpay, etc.). These services may collect:

  • Card number (encrypted)
  • Payment method
  • Billing information
  • Transaction history

c) Technical and Usage Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system and device type
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referral URLs and click behavior

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Processing and fulfilling food orders and reservations
  • Communicating order updates or responding to inquiries
  • Personalizing the user experience (e.g., saving preferences)
  • Sending promotional emails and offers (if opted-in)
  • Improving our website, services, and customer support
  • Detecting fraudulent activity and ensuring platform security
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information under certain circumstances:

  • Delivery Partners: Contact and delivery details may be shared to complete your order.
  • Payment Processors: Secure transmission of payment details to authorized gateways.
  • Marketing Tools: Email addresses may be shared with email service providers if you've opted in.
  • Legal Requests: Information may be shared if required by law or legal proceedings.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Store your site preferences
  • Understand how users interact with our site
  • Provide relevant content or offers
  • Measure website performance

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect some site features.

5. Data Security

  • SSL encryption for secure data transmission
  • Secure hosting infrastructure and firewalls
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular monitoring of potential security threats

Although we take strong precautions, no system is 100% secure. Users share data at their own risk.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request corrections or updates to your data
  • Request deletion of your data (where applicable)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • File a complaint if you believe your rights were violated

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@tastytrills.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites. We do not control or take responsibility for their privacy practices.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If a parent or guardian believes we’ve collected data from a child, please contact us to delete it.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted with the updated effective date.
